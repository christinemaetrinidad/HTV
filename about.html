About Health TrueValue Diagnostic Laboratory

Health True Value Diagnostic Laboratory is one of the highly recommended laboratory in Hagonoy, Bulacan, offers the high quality and low-cost of services from routine laboratory tests, imaging, and multi-specialty doctor.

Health True Value was established on September 8, 2018 by the Laboratory Owner and also the Laboratory Manager Mrs. Julie C. Santos. It is located at Unit 3&5 JC Bldg. Panganiban St. Poblacion, Hagonoy, Bulacan
